About me

Cruiser Command

Cruiser Command is a multiplayer strategy game created as a custom mod for Starcraft 2. It's a highly tactical game requiring a lot of teamwork to come out victorious over the other team.

I started working on it in 2011 using Starcraft 2's Galaxy Editor. Since then I have kept developing and maintaining it while communicating with the active playerbase about their thoughts and ideas.

Since it went public it has been played by thousands of players and maintained an average rating of over 4.7/5 from over 2000 reviews. The game has a loyal fanbase with some players having played the game for thousands of hours. There's also an active forum with 120 registered users.




SuperWebRecorder

During the spring of 2014 I spent several months working with 8 other KTH students on a project for the company The Beta Family. The Beta Family works with offering crowdsourced beta testing for companies by connecting a community of beta testers with a community of devlopers. To simplify this, we were tasked to create an application that records users' website experiences. This meant recording everything that happened on the site, following the user's path throughout the different pages of the site and recording the interactions. The recordings were then saved in a database to be replayed later on by the developer. It also included aggregation functions to combine replay data for visualizations such as heatmaps.

I was the project manager, but I also spent an active part on the programming group. It was a great experience and my first taste of programming in a slightly larger team. In the end, The Beta Family were very happy with the results and so were we.
